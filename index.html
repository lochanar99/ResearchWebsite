What we do

" EMAA is a mobile application which helps people to monitor their emotions and provide assistance. EMAA can be used by a patient or an individual to track their emotional state and will be able to obtain activity suggestions that will help them to change their current mood to a better state. If the state of the user is critical then support group members will be notified through a Simple Message Service (SMS) message.Healthcare practitioners can use the application to monitor the emotion status their patients."

Literature Survey

Emotions in human are expressed by different modes like facial expressions, body expressions, speech signal, and physiological signals. The mentioned modalities, independently or in various combinations are used as several approaches to emotion recognition. Although a vast number of studies have been carried out independently for the techniques mentioned above, the multimodal approach to identify the emotions of a user is a new research area in the current domain.

In the literature, various approaches to human emotion recognition have been mentioned by the surveys conducted. In the multimodal approach, in order to obtain an overall emotion level with the different modes, all the results should be fused. C. Zucco et al. proposed several fusion approaches by the application of sentimental analysis and affective computing methodologies for depression detection and monitoring in order to integrate information extracted from multiple modes of the system. Anil Kimar K.M. et al. proposed a feature level fusion technique and decision level techniques in the multimodal data analysis system. I.T. Meftah et al. have proposed a multimodal approach to recognize the basic emotions and also combining emotions like simulated and masked emotions by combining modalities like facial expressions, speech expressions, gestures and body gestures. Felipe Cid et al. proposed a novel multimodal emotion recognition system based on the visual and auditory information to detect five emotional states of humans.

Research Gap

Several types of studies have been done for the past few years about the systems that can monitor and assist emotions. After considering the previous studies, we identified the following limitations.

  • Predict an overall emotional state accurately.

    • Even though there have been past studies done regarding this particular area, one of the major limitations we identified was that there was no product that has the whole process in one. Each study had been done separately as individual modules for Facial Expression Analysis, Voice Expression Analysis, and Social Media and Text Content Analysis. To predict an overall emotional state accurately, these three subcomponents are used in our proposed system.

  • Customized activity suggestion.

    • After identifying the emotional state of the individual by the proposed system; customized activities which help for the individual to overcome from bad moods will be suggested by the proposed Assistive Application. These activities will be predicted respect to the cognition behavior of the user and this prediction will become more accurate with the time because the activity predicting the system will be trained and learned specific to the user. None of the previous systems addressed this feature.

  • Support Network.

    • Patient’s current state is not provided by some of the Emotion Monitoring Applications to caregivers such as health care provider and support group of the particular patient with more accurate manner. To address this issue, complete picture of individual’s state is provided and the health care provider can obtain a clear visibility over the effectiveness of treatment through the information provided by our system.

Research Problem

Emotion plays an important role in the human life. They express emotions in the day to day interactions. Emotions of an individual can change due to mental and physical circumstances. Throughout the day humans go through different types of emotions. Because of various reasons, some may subject to emotional disorders. Depression is a mood disorder characterized by persistently low mood and a feeling of sadness and loss of interest.And also a major cause of suicide is mental illness, very commonly depression. Over the past few years in Sri Lanka also considerable no. of deaths occurred due to depression.

Symptoms associated with mental states are observable by many modalities like facial expression analysis, vocal analysis, behaviour analysis, social media analysis and gesture analysis. In the current mobile application market though there are various types of emotion states detection applications none of them assesses the emotion detection/predictions accurately by analyzing major facts that involve displaying emotions.

The applications in the current app market offer some basic questionnaires to identify the symptoms of the user and provide some instructions to keep the mind of the users busy. It is essential to provide customized activity suggestions for the users, because of the diverse emotional levels and diverse methods of reactions displayed by different users.

For individuals with emotional disorders, constant monitoring and support are essential features. Healthcare practitioners can assess the emotional level of the diagnosed patient only at the time duration of the appointments. There is no possible way to assess it at home during a depressive episode. And if the individual is at a critical level, the danger signs cannot not be recognized and take immediate actions.

Research Objectives

Main Objectives

  • Implement a mobile application to provide continuous monitoring and support.
  • Implement a mobile application to recognize the overrall emotional level of a user, to predict the depression level using a multimodal approach.

Specific Objectives Objectives

  • Predict an accurate emotion level by analyzing the facial expression of the user.
  • Predict an accurate emotion level by analyzing voice of the particular person gives.
  • Predict an accurate emotion level by analyzing the social media content and text content of the particular user.
  • Learn the user behavior and give a better prediction of the emotional state of the user through the information extracted from the face analysis, voice analysis and the social media content and text content analysis.
  • User-specific effective activity prediction according to the emotional state of the user.
